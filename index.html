Hi, I’m Himanshu—a software engineer at Google passionate about building scalable systems, fostering creativity, and exploring the intersections of technology and education.

I'm an Indian immigrant who grew up in Hyderabad and New York City. At my core, I’m a builder and a lifelong learner. I take pride in understanding every detail of the systems I work with, putting in the effort needed to produce quality work that resonates with my values.

Here are some of the projects I've worked on:

  • YouTube's "Ask" Button

    I worked on the "Ask" button which lets YouTube premium users ask questions about any video.

    I worked on the complete end to end stack including web UI, backend, serving, reliability, generative AI, data quality, compliance, legal requirements, and android app. I also implemented specific features for the ask button to behave differently on educational and learning videos.

  • YouTube Quiz posts

    I led a small team to implement YouTube's Quiz post feature. This included the UI, backend, data pipeline, and reliability operations. We also implemented an exciting feature for creators to add quizzes on their videos.

    An example is this video which has quiz posts in the description.

  • GCP Logs Viewer

    I worked on large parts of the design, infrastructure, and implementation of the Google Cloud Platform logs viewer. This feature is used by a huge portion of businesses who use Google Cloud for their Developer workflows.

  • YouTube Courses

    I developed some of the core infrastructure for YouTube's courses platform. I specifically worked on a large part of the data pipeline, the reliability operations, and the UI for course videos.
