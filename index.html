Hi, I’m Himanshu—a software engineer at Google passionate about building scalable systems, fostering creativity, and exploring the intersections of technology and education.

I'm an Indian immigrant who grew up in Hyderabad and New York City. At my core, I’m a builder and a lifelong learner. I take pride in understanding every detail of the systems I work with, putting in the effort needed to produce quality work that resonates with my values.

My journey has been driven by curiosity, resilience, and a love for science. These values guide me to:

  • Stay positive and optimistic, even during tough times.
  • Be relentless in the pursuit of high quality.
  • Embrace individuality, even in the face of criticism.

Currently, I work at Google on features for YouTube that empower creators and learners. In my free time, I juggle hobbies like Crossfit, Poi spinning, and creating content for YouTube.
